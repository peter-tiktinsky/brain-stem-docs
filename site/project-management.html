Project management

This section covers the operational side of getting work done: how you organize a project, shape an idea into a plan, run that plan under safe human gates, and keep a single up-to-date view of every project's research and decisions. Each page covers one layer of that stack.

Process governance lives here. Write-time conventions (how individual files are shaped and governed at the moment they are created) live in Write-time governance.

Two kinds of governance — and where each lives

brain-stem splits its governance concerns into two categories so they stay separate and legible.

This section

Process governance

How work is organized and tracked over time: where project work lives, how plans are made and progressed, how execution is orchestrated safely, and how a project's knowledge rolls up into one view. The rules here are about workflow — how a multi-step effort moves from idea to verified.

Write-time governance section

Write-time governance

How each file is shaped the moment it is created: the frontmatter it requires, the folder it belongs in, the naming it must follow. These rules fire at write time to keep every file discoverable by Claude and findable by you — independent of any particular plan or project.
